⚠ Measurement alert · click metrics inflated by security bots
6 Chile campaigns report 100% CTR with 14–19K clicks on 3–5K opens — mathematically impossible unless each recipient clicked 4–6 times. Corporate security filters (Microsoft Defender, Proofpoint, Mimecast) automatically scan every inbound link for phishing detection, inflating click counts. Estimated true Chile CTR: 10–15%, not 60–100%. Spain shows clean CTR (4–30%) — audience with less aggressive scanning. Action: enable "Exclude automated clicks" in Brevo Settings → recalculate true performance.
